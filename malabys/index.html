About Malabys

Our story and our commitment to style and sustainability.

At Malabys, style meets purpose. We bring you globally loved fashion, trusted for quality and design, while making sure every choice you make with us creates a positive impact.

Here’s the reality: over 92 million tonnes of clothing waste end up in landfills every year, poisoning our environment and fueling climate change. We believe fashion shouldn’t cost the earth. That’s why we curate the best brands, premium thrifted pieces, and surplus clothing—delivered at prices your wallet will love.

The thrift fashion market in India is booming, already valued at USD 3.5 billion in 2024 and projected to grow at a 13.2% CAGR through 2033. What’s driving this? Rising youth demand, growing awareness of sustainable choices, social media influence, and the power of e-commerce. Thrift is no longer a niche—it’s the new mainstream.

When you choose Malabys, you’re not just buying clothes. You’re joining a movement to extend the life of garments, save water and energy, and cut down pollution. Every thrifted find is a small step towards a cleaner, greener planet—while keeping your style game strong.

Our Philosophy

  • We believe in sustainable fashion that respects both style and the planet.
  • Our team has a deep passion for fashion, driven by creativity and individuality.
  • We offer world-class fashion articles at prices that fit every budget.
  • Building trust with our customers is the foundation of everything we do.
